# Company Policies

## Cancellation Policy

### Appointment Cancellations

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our team.

**Free Cancellation Period:**
- Appointments can be cancelled free of charge if you notify us at least 24 hours before your scheduled time.

**Late Cancellations (Less than 24 hours):**
- Cancellations made within 24 hours of your appointment may incur a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the service cost.

**No-Shows:**
- If you do not show up for your appointment without notice, you may be charged the full cost of the service.

**How to Cancel:**
1. Use our chatbot with your booking reference number
2. Call (555) 123-4567
3. Email cancellations@example.com
4. Log into your account on our website

### Rescheduling

You may reschedule your appointment at any time without penalty, subject to availability. We recommend rescheduling at least 24 hours in advance.

## Refund Policy

### Service Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services. If you are not satisfied:

**Full Refunds:**
- Available within 7 days of your service
- Must contact us with your concerns
- Applies to services where expectations were not met

**Partial Refunds:**
- Considered on a case-by-case basis
- Depends on the nature of the service and circumstances

**Non-Refundable:**
- Services that have been fully rendered and completed
- No-show appointments
- Cancellations within the penalty period

### How to Request a Refund

1. Contact us within 7 days of your service
2. Explain the reason for your request
3. Our team will review and respond within 3 business days
4. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days
